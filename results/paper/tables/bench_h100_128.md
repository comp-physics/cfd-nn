# Cost Comparison: 128³ (2.1M cells) on H100 80GB

All models: channel flow, RK3, skew scheme, FFT Poisson.

| Model | ms/step | Turb (ms) | Features (ms) | Inference (ms) | Overhead | Mcells/s |
|-------|---------|-----------|---------------|----------------|----------|----------|
| Baseline | 4.84 | — | — | — | — | 433 |
| SST | 4.97 | 0.025 | — | — | +3% | 422 |
| TBNN | 24.96 | 20.03 | ~11 | ~9 | +415% | 84 |
| TBRF-1 | 44.69 | 39.74 | 11.1 | 2.6 | +822% | 47 |
| TBRF-5 | 57.41 | 52.48 | 10.9 | 15.8 | +1085% | 37 |
| TBRF-10 | 81.40 | 76.44 | 10.9 | 39.3 | +1580% | 26 |

Key observations:
- Feature computation (invariants + tensor basis) is ~11 ms regardless of model
  (dominated by GPU→CPU sync for tree traversal)
- TBRF tree inference scales linearly with tree count: 2.6 → 15.8 → 39.3 ms
- TBNN is 5x baseline; TBRF-10 is 17x baseline
- SST transport equations add only 3% overhead
